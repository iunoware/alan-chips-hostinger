Rooted in Tradition Since 1960

  • Began over 66 years ago in Kerala, pioneering plantain chips for commercial production.
  • Introduced a new standard in thin-sliced plantain, potato, tapioca, and jackfruit chips.
  • Established in June 1960 as SAN Chips, Courtallam, earning deep customer trust.
  • Today, Alan Chips in Ilanji, Tenkasi District, is known for freshly prepared, authentic chips.
  • The name Alan Chips stands for one promise: a delicious snack, made the right way.

— Alan Food Company

Our Heritage

A Legacy of
Tradition & Taste

Founded over six decades ago, Alan Chips represents a journey rooted in authentic taste and uncompromising quality. What began as a family-led effort to craft better plantain chips soon became a name people trusted for consistency, freshness, and flavor.

Using time-honored methods and carefully selected ingredients, every chip is prepared with attention to detail. Each batch reflects the same values that have guided us since 1960 — authenticity, care, and respect for taste.
